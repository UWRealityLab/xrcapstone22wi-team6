About Team Sonic Bloom

What is Sonic Bloom?

Sonic Bloom is a virtual fly simulator, where you can learn how to fly with the two controllers and have ability to check for safe landings, do different maneuvers. We hope to the users with no previous experience in flying and VR can easily get used to flying and have fun!
